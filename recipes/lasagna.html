Lasagna

Description

This lasagna is designed for those busy weeknights where you just don't have the time to get an hour of cooking in. It's a simple, elegant dish which maintains all the things you love about a standard lasagna recipe, while being less time-intensive and requiring less strenuous effort to complete. This is an excellent lasagna, 10/10.

Ingredients

  • Cheese.
  • Those pasta things you use for lasagna.
  • Sauce

Steps

  1. Get the ingredients out.
  2. Add the ingredients together.
  3. Throw the thing in the oven till finished.
