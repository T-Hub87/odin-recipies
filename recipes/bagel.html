Bagel

Description

This bagel is a delicious way to get your morning started, and will give you the energy to go out and conquer the day.

Ingredients

  • Bagel
  • Bagel seasoning
  • Whatever your heart desires.

Steps

  1. Bake bagel.
  2. Season with everything bagel seasoning.
  3. Add whatever you like, cream cheese, butter, etc.
